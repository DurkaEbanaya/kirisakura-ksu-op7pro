How it works

  • WiFi Hotspot: Creates a concurrent AP. Your WiFi stays connected.
  • USB / BT Tether: Enable tethering in Android Settings, then grant VPN per device here.
  • VPN Grant: Routes that device's traffic through the phone's VPN tunnel (tun0).
  • Framework Fix: If you enable Android's built-in hotspot, this module automatically converts it to concurrent AP and reconnects WiFi.
